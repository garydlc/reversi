Child Life Specialist questions [30 minutes]



General questions: 



  1. Let’s talk a bit about you - can you talk to me a bit about what you do?

  2. Can you describe your hospital briefly?

    1. How many patients are you responsible for at your hospital?

    2. How many other child life specialists are there?

  4. How does a typical day look like in your role? Walk me through it in as much detail as you can remember.

  5. In the last few weeks, what were some highlights? Can you provide some context?

    1. What made these experiences a highlight for you?

  7. In the last few weeks, what would you say were some of the challenges you faced? Again, can you provide some context on what happened?

    1. What made these situations challenging?

    2. How did you deal with these challenges?



Focused on Zott:

  1. Do you Interact with Zott CMS?

  2. IF YES: how does that look like?

  3. When do you typically use it?

  4. What do you do you? IF not brought up organically, prompt:

    1. Have you ever added content to the CMS?

      1. What do you recall about your experience?

    3. Have you ever removed content from your hospitals CMS instance?

      1. Why did you have to do this?

      2. What do you recall about your experience?

  6. How has your experience been using it?

    1. What are some challenges you’ve experienced using it?

    2. What about highlights?

  8. Has there been a time when a patient saw something that they shouldn’t have seen?

    1. If so, what happened? Can you talk a bit about this situation?

    2. How was this resolved?
